Custom Programming & IT Solutions Tailored to Your Needs

At Knight Group, we specialize in delivering customized programming and IT solutions to tackle your unique business challenges. Whether you're looking to build a custom web application to avoid costly monthly CMS fees, automate repetitive tasks to save time, optimize your systems for better performance, manage your data more efficiently, scale your business operations with strategic development, or simply want a cool functional script for personal common tasks. We provide solutions that align with your goals. Our extensive experience spans front-end and back-end development, workflow automation, and data-driven optimization—ensuring your business operates seamlessly and efficiently. Have a unique request? Contact us, and we'll craft a solution tailored to your needs.

Custom SQL & Database Management: With over a decade of experience in SQL development and query optimization, we have built and maintained databases for e-commerce platforms and business intelligence solutions. Leveraging T-SQL, MySQL, and Microsoft SQL Server, we design tailored data solutions for performance tuning, automated reporting, and business insights that help drive informed decision-making.
End-to-End Web Development: Our experience includes building scalable e-commerce stores from scratch, such as one of our current projects, ManCaves.Store, using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. We incorporate Firebase and Snipcart for secure authentication and checkout processes, dynamic product loading, using local history to display previously viewed or purchased products, and delivering responsive, SEO-optimized solutions that enhance user experience and drive conversions.
Python Automation: We have utilized Python and Selenium to automate repetitive business tasks such as data scraping, product catalog generation from CSV files, and API integration for marketing platforms. Our automation solutions increase efficiency and allow businesses to focus on strategic growth while minimizing manual workloads.
PowerShell Automation Solutions: Our custom PowerShell scripts have been successfully implemented for automating IT administrative tasks, including managing system configurations, batch file processing, and optimizing workflows. Notable projects include automating resolution changes for applications and automated file renaming for video recordings based on event triggers.
Seamless API Integration & Development: We have extensive experience integrating Google Merchant Center, Google Ads, Local Listings, Business Profile, Analytics, and Tag Manager to optimize business operations and increase online visibility. Our custom API solutions allow businesses to connect multiple platforms for seamless data exchange and operational efficiency.
Data Analysis & Business Intelligence: We specialize in transforming raw data into actionable insights using SQL, Python, and visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI. Our past projects include building interactive dashboards for tracking sales performance and analyzing market trends, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions with confidence.
