Vincent Knight: Owner & Expert Handyman

Vincent Knight brings over a decade of experience as a journeyman plumber, having worked with Roto Rooter in Pittsburgh, PA. His extensive expertise includes commercial building maintenance, sewer line repairs, camera inspections, and large-scale excavation projects. From plumbing repairs and water heater installations to complete sewer system overhauls—he's done it all. Beyond plumbing, Vincent has over 15 years of experience in home repair and remodeling, managing properties across Pinellas County through our family’s property management business. In addition to home services, he is skilled in automotive repairs, specializing in fixing and replacing auto parts such as alternators, starters, and brakes. Whether it's home improvements or vehicle repairs, Vincent is the trusted professional for all your handyman needs.

How We Work

At Knight Group Handyman Services, we follow a straightforward process to ensure your satisfaction:

  • Step 1: Call or email us to schedule an appointment. Vincent will come to your property to assess the work needed and provide a free, no-obligation estimate.
  • Step 2: We draft a contract detailing the scope of the work, estimated timeline, and cost.
  • Step 3: After approval, work begins! Payments are typically split into a deposit at the start and the remainder upon completion. We accept PayPal, Venmo, and most major credit cards.
  • Step 4: Upon completion, we walk you through the work done and ensure you're satisfied with the results before final payment is made.
  • General Pricing: Standard jobs start at $150 for the first hour of work, with an hourly rate of $75 thereafter.
