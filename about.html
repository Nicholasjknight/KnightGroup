About Knight Group

Our Mission

At Knight Group, our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable, and affordable services that cater to both homeowners and businesses. We strive to offer comprehensive handyman solutions and innovative custom development services, ensuring our clients receive tailored solutions that enhance their spaces and operations. Whether it’s a home repair, an auto service, or a technology solution, we are committed to delivering excellence and building long-term relationships based on trust and satisfaction.

Our Story

Originally established in 1995 in Safety Harbor, Florida, Knight Group started as a computer networking company providing mainframe and server management for clients such as Ruth Eckerd Hall and the Safety Harbor Spa. Over time, as technology evolved, we expanded our focus to include database management, IT consulting, and custom programming solutions to help businesses optimize their operations.

Recognizing the growing need for property management services, we leveraged our success in IT to invest in and manage properties throughout Pinellas County. This transition led to the development of our handyman services, helping homeowners and businesses with maintenance, renovations, and general repairs.

Relaunching Knight Group Handyman Services LLC

In 2024, we restructured Knight Group to better meet the demands of our clients, launching Knight Group Handyman Services LLC. Our experienced team now offers comprehensive home repair and improvement solutions, alongside specialized auto repair services for customers seeking reliable, cost-effective solutions.

Our lead handyman, with extensive experience as a journeyman plumber with Roto Rooter in Pennsylvania, brings a wealth of industry knowledge. While we do not currently hold a general contractor’s license in Florida, we adhere to state regulations by providing services that do not require permits and partnering with licensed professionals when necessary.

Our handyman and auto services include:

  • General home repairs and maintenance
  • Plumbing repairs and installations
  • Drywall installation and patching
  • Small-scale remodeling projects
  • Installing fixtures and fans
  • Auto part replacements (alternators, starters, brakes, etc.)
  • Can do it all!

We are fully insured and hold workers' compensation exemptions to provide our clients with peace of mind.

Knight Group Custom Development & IT Solutions

In parallel with our handyman services, we continue to grow our technology offerings through Knight Group Custom Development & IT Solutions. We provide businesses and individuals with tailored solutions that range from web development to remote IT support and automation.

Our technology services include:

  • Custom web development and application design
  • Database design and optimization
  • Remote IT support and troubleshooting
  • API integrations and workflow automation
  • Cloud solutions and data security

Whether you're looking to optimize your business operations, improve your online presence, or secure your digital assets, Knight Group has the expertise to deliver innovative and scalable solutions.

Our Commitment to Excellence

We are committed to delivering high-quality services and adapting to the evolving needs of our clients. Our comprehensive approach, combining practical experience with technical expertise, ensures that we offer a wide range of solutions under one trusted brand.

At Knight Group, customer satisfaction is our priority. We work closely with each client to understand their needs and provide solutions that are both effective and affordable.

Looking to the Future

As we continue to expand our services, our vision is to become the go-to provider for handyman and custom development services in Pinellas County and beyond. We aim to provide innovative, efficient, and high-quality solutions that empower our clients to focus on what matters most.

Thank you for considering Knight Group. We look forward to serving you with integrity, professionalism, and a commitment to excellence.
